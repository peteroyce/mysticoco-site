How to Use Mysti CoCo

Always patch‑test on skin and follow your doctor’s advice, especially for babies and sensitive skin.

Cooking

Use a spoonful for sautéing, tempering or baking where you would normally use cooking oil, for a gentle coconut aroma and light finish.

Hair

Warm slightly and massage into the scalp and hair lengths 30–45 minutes before washing for a soft, nourished finish and natural shine.

Skin

Apply a thin layer on slightly damp skin after a shower to lock in moisture, or use as an overnight hand and foot mask on dry areas.

Baby Care*

For baby massage, always consult your paediatrician and test on a small patch first. Discontinue if any redness or irritation appears.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this oil edible?

Mysti CoCo virgin coconut milk oil is intended for edible use. Always follow the directions on your product label and consult your healthcare provider if you have dietary concerns.

How should I store the oil?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight with the cap tightly closed. Coconut oil may solidify in cooler temperatures; this is natural. Liquify by placing the bottle in warm water.

What is the shelf life?

Please refer to the “Best before” date printed on each bottle. Update this answer with exact shelf‑life once your packaging is final.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

Many people with dry or sensitive skin enjoy coconut oil, but reactions are individual. Patch‑test on the inner arm, wait 24 hours, and discontinue use if irritation occurs.
