How to Use Mysti CoCo

Always patch‑test on skin and follow your doctor’s advice, especially for babies and sensitive skin.

Cooking

Use a spoonful for sautéing, tempering or baking where you would normally use cooking oil, for a gentle coconut aroma.

Hair

Warm slightly and massage into the scalp and hair lengths 30–45 minutes before washing for a soft, nourished finish.

Skin

Apply a thin layer on slightly damp skin after a shower to lock in moisture or use as an overnight hand and foot mask.

Baby Care*

Can be used as a massage oil only after consulting your paediatrician and checking for allergies. Always patch‑test first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this oil edible?

Mysti CoCo virgin coconut milk oil is intended for edible use. Follow the directions on your product label and consult your healthcare provider if you have dietary concerns.

How should I store the oil?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight with the cap tightly closed. Coconut oil may solidify in cooler temperatures; this is natural.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?
